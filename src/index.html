About Our Mess

Project Cafecito is the brainchild of Soumit Salman Rahman and Daniel Oliver Vidaud, two tech geeks who thought, “Why not make life easier for people who actually work for a living?” We're building AI tools to handle the soul-crushing parts of running a business. We’re a bootstrapped outfit - cause VCs can eat it.

Soumit’s the guy who’s built some stuff for Microsoft and Aurora Innovation, while Daniel’s the aerospace nerd who made Boeing planes less chaotic. Together, we’re trying to save you from the horrors of paperwork and bad workflows. No promises, but we’re giving it a shot.
